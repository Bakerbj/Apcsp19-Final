Random Story Generator

Our mission here at ebbuvkeau4owaiun industries is to prevent both boredom and writer's block. By giving a completely random story, we will inspire readers to create their own stories, and with a litte work, they may even become almost as good of writers as this computer. Maybe.

This website was created by a nonwriter for literally anybody to read.

This website is here to achieve multiple purposes. The first two of course are to prevent boredom, useful in keeping people from doing stupid things for entertainment, and to prevent writers block, by giving creative directions for authors to take their stories into. In addition, this website promotes reading, which is far too uncommon in today's society. By providing humorous situations, users will develop good feelings about reading, and want to read more often.
